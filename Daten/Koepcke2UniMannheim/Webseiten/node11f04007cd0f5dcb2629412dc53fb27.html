The Samsung Galaxy S6 phone is a great choice as your new digital companion with awesome performance and striking design. Its beautiful build perfectly marries glass and metal, producing an artwork-like phone that's easy on your eyes and comfortable in your hand. The octa-core processor and gigantic amount of memory keep your OS and apps running smoothly. Take great selfies with the 5MP front camera, and capture gorgeous stills and 4K videos with the 16MP rear camera powered by Optical Image Stabilization technology. Enjoy all your photos and movies with vibrant color and subtle details on the 5.1" Super AMOLED display with 2560 x 1440 resolution. Log in instantly with the fingerprint scanner, no more password required. A non-removable 2550mAh battery with fast charging and wireless charging support delivers hours of entertainment before charging is needed.

Exquisitely Crafted, Captivatingly Brilliant

Inspired by the works of glassblowers and artisan metalsmiths, the Samsung Galaxy S6 represents a seamless fusion of glass and metal. Make a breathtaking design statement with its beautiful curves and radiant glass surfaces that reflect a wide spectrum of dazzling colors.

Peerless Performance

With twice the booting speed of previous models and super-fast app launching, the Samsung Galaxy S6 gives you unbeatable performance and improved energy efficiency. Its 64-bit architecture is truly powerful, letting you take multitasking and multimedia functions to the next level.

Dazzling Display

Equipped with a 5.1” Quad HD (2560 X 1440) Super AMOLED display, the Samsung Galaxy S6 provides an impeccably sharp viewing experience. Featuring an adaptive display, it provides unbeatable clarity both indoors and outdoors. Plus, improved brightness levels and pixel density let you take superb images wherever you are.

Improved Aperture, Clearer Pictures

The Galaxy S6 powers your selfies with a 5MP front-facing camera and a wide-angle lens, allowing you to capture more of the background and all of your friends. The ultra-responsive 16MP rear-facing camera with built-in Optical Image Stabilization and Auto HDR will help you take great shots anywhere, every time. Both of the Samsung Galaxy S6’s front and rear cameras feature F1.9 aperture, allowing you to take clearer images in low light situation. What's more, simply press the home button twice to go straight into camera mode – so you'll never miss an important moment again.

Fast Charging and Power Saving

Equipped with a 2550 mAh Fast Charging battery, Samsung’s exclusive Ultra Power Saving Mode and embedded wireless charging capabilities, the Galaxy S6 keeps you connected to the things you love. With fast charging, your battery goes from zero to up to 50% in about 30 minutes. And in Ultra Power Saving Mode, at only 10% power, you’ll still get calls and texts for up to 24 hours. (Wireless charging pad sold separately.)

Enhanced Security

Enjoy unprecedented peace of mind with the Samsung Galaxy S6’s powerful security solutions: KNOX, a fingerprint scanner.

Smart Manager

The new Smart Manager application lets you check an array of useful information such as battery status, storage, RAM, security and more at a glance.

Pay Almost Anywhere You Can Swipe or Tap Your Card

Introducing Samsung Pay, the mobile payment service that works virtually anywhere you can swipe your card — from local grocery stores to corner coffee shops to your favorite department store. It’s also highly secure and simple to use with your Galaxy S6. (Only compatible with select cards from participating banks/issuers, participating carriers and certain Samsung devices.)
