    THE NEW MOMENTUM ON-EAR

    The new Sennheiser MOMENTUM On-Ear takes passion for perfect sound to a new level. It combines high-resolution sound with a lightweight, puristic design and exclusive materials like stainless steel and AlcantaraA. With countless improvements, technological innovations and the collective MOMENTUM of our developers, we now present to you, the state-of-the-art MOMENTUM On-Ear.

    DETAILED AND CLEAR

    The new generation of MOMENTUM On-Ear headphones carves out every aural nuance in even finer detail. Thanks to proprietary 18-I Neodymium transducers that make the trademark MOMENTUM sound an even more brilliant experience. Clear, richly detailed, and with a powerful bass.

    PURISTIC AND DURABLE

    The new MOMENTUM On-Ear not only excels with improved sound but also with its revised design. The slightly curved profile of the headband as well as high-class materials like breathable AlcantaraA make for enhanced wearing comfort.

    COMFORTABLE AND VARIABLE

    The ear cups of the new MOMENTUM On-Ear are attached to highly mobile ball joints running steplessly in ergonomically shaped and collapsible sliders. The result: The headphones fit perfectly, also in their new space-saving carrying bag, thanks to the collapsible design. Headband and ear cushions of the MOMENTUM On-Ear are covered with super soft, breathable AlcantaraA. For still more wearing comfort the ear cushions are filled with a special two-layer foam

    AESTHETIC AND MOBILE

    With its low profile the angled 3.5-mm stereo jack plug is optimized for the use on mobile devices. Fitted with jack plugs on both ends, the cable is completely detachable and can be replaced if necessary.

    HANDY AND INTUITIVE

    The integrated in-line remote comfortably lets you control phone calls, music, and volume. Furthermore, it supports Apple devices like iPod, iPhone, and iPad as well as Android tablets and smartphones.
