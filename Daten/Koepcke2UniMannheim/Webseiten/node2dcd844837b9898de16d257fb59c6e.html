Technical Features

The HTC One-M7 mobile phone features a large 4.7 inch touchscreen with HTC Sense v5 user interface. The phone has a powerful Quad-core 1.7 GHz Krait 300 processor. The phone has a 4.0 Ultrapixel rear camera and a secondary 2.1 megapixel front facing camera. Addtional features include HSDPA+ network and wi-fi direct. It supports DLNA® for wirelessly streaming media from the phone to a compatible TV or computer.

Rating: 5/5Happy with the Product and the Seller

This review is from: HTC One M7 32GB / 801s Silver 4.7" Touch Screen 4.0 Ultrapixel Camera Unlocked GSM Mobile Phone

Pros: Nice solid feel to the phone. Easy transfer of data and settings since I moved from an Android to an Android. Video and sound quality (including call quality) is outstanding.

Cons: Battery life is not quite as good as I would expect from a phone that puts so much emphasis on video and sound but it seems to be getting better rather than worse (is that even possible?)

Other Thoughts: Item arrived timely and in fine condition. Easy set up and transfer of data with backup. Very, very happy with the product and the seller. Be sure and get the HTC car charger since the generic chargers don't seem to work that well with the phone.
