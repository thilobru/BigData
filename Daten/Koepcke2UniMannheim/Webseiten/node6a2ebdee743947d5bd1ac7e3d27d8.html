What is the 3D phone case?

 

 

3D phone case is the plastic case with the lenticular sheet with image printed(need the special design for the image). As the lenticular have many small cylinder, and the refraction theory of the cylinder makes your left eye and right eye see two images from different angles at the same time, so your brain will integrate them into a 3d depth image. (The theory of flash effect is same)
