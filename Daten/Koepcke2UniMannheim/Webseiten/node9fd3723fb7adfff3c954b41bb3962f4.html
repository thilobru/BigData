Description

Beautifully crafted from metal and glass, Samsung Galaxy S6 edge delivers a revolutionary experience while featuring the world’s first dual-curved glass display.

Finally, a smartphone that’s more than simply beautiful. It’s better. The Samsung Galaxy S® 6 edge is reinvented from the outside in. Beneath stunning metal and sophisticated glass lies our fastest, most powerful processor ever in a smartphone. Our best HD display yet brings your life to light—even in sunlight. And the dynamic, ultra-responsive camera captures the true-to-life brilliance of any moment. Every detail. Every day. Meet the reimagined Samsung Galaxy S® 6 edge.

Beauty in Design

The Galaxy S 6 edge’s stunning metal bezel cascades into sophisticated glass to create our slimmest, most-lightweight device. It’s our most revolutionary design yet.

A Screen Like You’ve Never Seen

Our 5.1" Quad HD Super AMOLED® display automatically adapts to the content you’re viewing. Our brightest screen yet, it makes documents, images and videos pop—even in sunlight. The Galaxy S 6 edge delivers exceptional brilliance, no matter what the conditions.

Powerful Performance

Powered by the Samsung Exynos® 7420 Octa-core 64-bit processor, the new Galaxy S 6 edge gives you the most speed and best performance of any Samsung smartphone. Browse, work, watch and game—faster than ever, all at the same time.
