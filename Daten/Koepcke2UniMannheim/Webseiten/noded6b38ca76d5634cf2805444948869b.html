      Fosmon HYBO-SD features a dual layer build made out of high-quality polycarbonate (PC) and soft silicone that helps guard your Google Nexus 6P / Huawei Nexus 6P against scratches, dirt, minor drops, and bumps. HYBO-SD fuses two different materials to protect your Google Nexus 6P / Huawei Nexus 6P while offering multiple color combinations. With interchangeable layers, why settle with one color combo? Combine the different colors and the bedazzled polycarbonate (PC) backplate to make the perfect combination that matches your style! Uniquely crafted screen lip that lifts your device's screen from the surface when placed face down. Fosmon HYBO-SD is revised with precision cut-outs to ensure full access to all ports, buttons, and features of your Google Nexus 6P / Huawei Nexus 6P. The sturdy form-fitted design of the HYBO-SD provides optimal protection measuring at 6mm and weighs less than 2oz.
