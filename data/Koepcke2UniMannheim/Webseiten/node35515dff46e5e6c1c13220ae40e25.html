  • by nodoors4Jan 7, 2016Verified purchase - NEW

    great sound but not the easiest to use

    I was frustrated with my learning curve on how to best insert the ear buds. It took me several days and was a frustrating experience. I almost sent them back. But now I've mastered it and it is not a problem.
    I am glad I stuck with it. The sound is incredible. I had been using Klipsch and most recently Etyomic that were about $125. The 425s give me about twice as much music, a much more expansive sound and great soundstage or presence.
    Over the years in auditioning stereo electronics and speakers(anybody remember Vandersteen?) and now headphones, my benchmark recording is Steve Winwood's Back in the High Life Again, especially the drum intro, and more recently added Jack White's Lazarretto. I hear sounds on the 425s that I did not hear on the others. The overall listening experience was significantly improved.
    I did not audition other earbuds in this price range, these were recommended by a knowledgeable friend. I am very pleased with the purchase.    Read full review
